§ 01 · The challenge

The decisions are urgent.
The data is not ready.

These are the conditions that drive organizations to engage our practice. They are not technology problems. They are infrastructure problems, and they compound every quarter.

01 · Reporting

Reporting delays that stall executive decisions.

Your finance team spends days pulling data from multiple systems, reconciling in spreadsheets, and delivering numbers that are outdated on arrival. Leadership cannot act because the reporting cycle is slower than the business cycle.

02 · Margin

Margin erosion that cannot be quantified.

Revenue is known. Total cost is known. But margin by product line, by customer, by provider, by matter, by region cannot be determined because the data is fragmented across systems that were never designed to talk to each other.

03 · Legacy

Legacy ERP creating operational and security risk.

Systems like AX 2012, GP, and NAV are past end-of-life with no security patches being issued. Every month without a migration strategy compounds the risk to operations, data integrity, and compliance posture.

04 · People

High-value people absorbed by low-value work.

Analysts copying data between systems. Controllers rebuilding the same workbooks every month. Your most capable people spending the majority of their time on manual processes that should have been automated years ago.

05 · Truth

No single version of the truth.

Sales, operations, and finance each report different numbers for the same metric. Without a governed, authoritative data layer, executive alignment is impossible. Every meeting begins with reconciling whose numbers are right.

06 · AI

AI and forecasting ambitions without the foundation.

Demand forecasting, anomaly detection, and intelligent automation all require clean, structured, governed data as input. Without the right data architecture in place, AI initiatives produce noise rather than insight.

§ 04 · The Coolidge Method

Three layers.
Raw to ready.

Every engagement follows the same proven architecture pattern. Raw data enters at the base. Trusted, auditable insight emerges at the top.

Layer 01 · Bronze
Capture.

Capture

We connect to your existing systems (ERP, CRM, billing, clinical, operational) and ingest data automatically on a schedule. Nothing changes in your day-to-day operations. Source data is preserved exactly as-is for auditability and compliance.

Layer 02 · Silver
Validate.

Validate

Data is cleaned, standardized, and verified so every department operates from the same numbers. Duplicates removed. Errors flagged. Business rules applied consistently. Finance and operations see the same truth.

Layer 03 · Gold
Deliver.

Deliver

Clean, trusted data is organized for the people who need it. Executives receive margin and revenue dashboards. Managers receive operational reports. Analysts receive self-service access. AI models receive the structured inputs required to generate forecasts and recommendations.
