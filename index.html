Our approach

The Strata methodology.

Every engagement follows the same proven pattern. Raw data flows in at the base. Trusted, actionable insight emerges at the top.

Layer 01 / Bronze

Capture

We connect to your existing systems and pull data automatically on a schedule. Nothing changes in your day-to-day operations. Your source data is preserved exactly as-is for auditability.

Layer 02 / Silver

Validate

We clean, standardize, and verify the data so every department is working from the same numbers. Duplicates are removed, errors are flagged, and business rules are applied consistently.

Layer 03 / Gold

Deliver

Clean, trusted data is organized for the people who need it: executives get dashboards, managers get reports, analysts get self-service access, and AI models get the structured inputs they require.
